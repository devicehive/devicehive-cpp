#define HIVE_HAS_RVALUE_REFS

Enable rvalue references.

If your compiler is modern enough, you can define HIVE_HAS_RVALUE_REFS macro to enable move contructors and move assignment operators. This may increase the execution speed for some data type by eliminating copying.
